The

of

trade

of
growers
and
processors
of
Wild
Blueberries
from
Maine,
Quebec
and
Eastern
Canada,
dedicated
to
bringing
the
Wild
Blueberry
health
story
and
unique
Wild
Advantages
to
consumers
and
the
trade
worldwide.
By
creating
the
Wild
About
Blueberries
blog,
we
aim
to
broaden
the
conversation
about
nutrition,
wellness,
healthy
aging,
disease
prevention,
and
how
nutritious
foods
like
wild
blueberries
can
enhance
our
lives,
our
health
and
our
plates.
